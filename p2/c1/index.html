Project 1 Topics

Topic 1: Funky Desk lamps

This store sells fun and funky desk lamps that you've never seen before.

Topic 2: Elixir Emporium

A whimsical online potion shop where customers can purchase consumable potions and spells. Things that would be sold would include: bottled substances such as drinks that would let you sleep instantly, make your nails grow longer, hair turn pink etc. There will also be edible things such as candy.

Topic 3: Special Gum Store

This is a gum store where you can find unique gum from other countries or even planets. These gums are not only unique and bizarre looking, but also taste like nothing you have ever experienced before.
